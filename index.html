üìù Supplementary Retirement Scheme (SRS)

One of the most underused tax-saving tools in Singapore.

Tax Benefits

  • Tax deductions on contributions
  • Investment returns are tax-free
  • Only 50% taxable at retirement
  • Contribution deadline: 31 Dec

2026 Contribution Limits

  • Singaporeans/PRs: S$16,000/yr
  • Foreigners: S$35,700/yr
  • Combine with CPF for max benefits
  • Opens investment window

üí° Pro Tip

Max out your SRS contribution every year to reduce taxable income while building a diversified retirement portfolio outside of CPF.

üí° Key Strategies

5

Tax Optimization at Retirement

Strategic withdrawals can minimize your tax burden in retirement.

Withdrawal Strategy

  • Withdrawal Age: 62 (retirement) to avoid 5% penalty
  • Taxable Amount: Only 50% of SRS withdrawal is taxed
  • Annual Withdrawals: Spread withdrawals to stay in lower tax bracket
  • CPF Transfer: Can transfer SRS to CPF for retirement income
  • Minimum Sum: Withdraw up to S$40,000 annually penalty-free

‚ö†Ô∏è Important SRS Rules

  • Early Withdrawal Penalty: Before 62 = 5% penalty (except for medical reasons)
  • Account Closure: Must close SRS account by age 63
  • Minimum Withdrawal: Must withdraw at least S$4,000 per year after age 62
  • Cancer Insurance: Can use SRS for ElderShield/CareShield premiums
