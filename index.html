üìù Supplementary Retirement Scheme (SRS)

One of the most underused tax-saving tools in Singapore.

Tax Benefits

  • Tax deductions on contributions
  • Investment returns are tax-free
  • Only 50% taxable at retirement
  • Contribution deadline: 31 Dec

2026 Contribution Limits

  • Singaporeans/PRs: S$16,000/yr
  • Foreigners: S$35,700/yr
  • Combine with CPF for max benefits
  • Opens investment window

üí° Pro Tip

Max out your SRS contribution every year to reduce taxable income while building a diversified retirement portfolio outside of CPF.
